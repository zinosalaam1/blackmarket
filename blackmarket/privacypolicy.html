01 / Information We Collect

What lands in the file

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Password — stored in encrypted/hashed form by our backend provider; we never see or store it in plain text
  • Username / display name

Gameplay Data

To run the core game, we store:

  • In-game score and progress
  • In-game currency balance ("coins")
  • Purchase history related to in-game items (see Section 04)

Automatically Collected

Our backend provider may log limited technical details automatically — IP address, device type, and activity timestamps — strictly for security and reliability purposes.

What We Don't Touch

✕ Camera
✕ Precise or approximate location
✕ Contacts
✕ Photos & microphone

The app requests no permissions beyond internet access.

02 / How We Use Your Information

Why we keep it on file

We use the information above to:

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Save and sync your progress, score, and in-game currency across sessions
  • Process in-app purchases
  • Maintain the security and integrity of the app
  • Respond to support requests
  • Improve and troubleshoot the app
Standing policy

We do not sell your personal information. Full stop.

03 / Third-Party Service Providers

Who else has access

Supabase — backend infrastructure, authentication, and database hosting. Supabase processes and stores your account and gameplay data on our behalf, under its own security and data-processing practices.

[IF APPLICABLE: Google Play Billing handles payment processing for in-app purchases — we do not receive or store your card details.]

We'll update this section if we add or change providers.

04 / In-App Purchases

Coins & transactions

If the app offers in-game currency or item purchases, those transactions run through the app store platform (e.g., Google Play). We receive confirmation of the purchase and update your in-game balance — we never collect or store your payment card or billing details ourselves.

05 / Data Storage and Security

How the file is kept

Your information lives on secure servers operated by our backend provider. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect it from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we can't guarantee absolute security.

06 / Data Retention

How long the file stays open

We retain your account and gameplay information for as long as your account stays active, or as needed to provide the app's services. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time — see Section 11.

07 / Children's Privacy

Age restrictions

The app is not directed to children under 13 (or the relevant minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under that age. If you believe a child has given us personal information, contact us so we can remove it.

08 / Your Rights and Choices

What you can request

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent to processing, where applicable

To exercise any of these, reach us using the details in Section 11.

09 / Data Sharing and Disclosure

Where the file travels

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share it:

  • With service providers who help us run the app (e.g., Supabase), under confidentiality obligations
  • If required by law, legal process, or government request
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of business assets
10 / Changes to This Policy

Reopening the file

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Rev" date at the top of this document, and where appropriate, we'll give additional notice inside the app.
